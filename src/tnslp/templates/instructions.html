Exploration

The goal of the game is to escape The No-Shoelace Place. To escape you must explore the ward to find items and locations that you can use. Some of these items will be readily available for you to use, while others may be locked away or hidden. As you explore, you will be rewarded experience points (xp) for finding items and completing objectives, which will be important as you progress through the game.
